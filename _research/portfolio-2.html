--- title: "Mental Health and Forced Displacement" excerpt: "Investigating mental health support technologies for refugees and displaced populations, with a focus on gender considerations." collection: research ---

This research stream examines how technology can support mental health interventions for refugees and displaced populations, with particular attention to gender dynamics.

Research Focus

  • Understanding mental health needs in displacement contexts
  • Designing culturally appropriate digital interventions
  • Gender-sensitive approaches to mental health technology
  • Community-based participatory research methods

Current Projects

Working with community organizations to develop and evaluate mental health support tools that are accessible, culturally appropriate, and sustainable.
