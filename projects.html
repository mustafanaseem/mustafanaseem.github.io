Following is a summary of projects I'm currently working on and have worked on:

Water ATM: A Water Dispensing and Quality Measurement Unit

WaterATM is a user friendly automated water dispensing machine installed over filtration plants. It regulates usage of clean drinking water by using a user authentication mechanism. It provides technological innovations and help in saving clean and filtered water wastage which eventually impacts the community health.
